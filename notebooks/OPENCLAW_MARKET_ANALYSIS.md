## China Market Analysis

### Market Characteristics

- Large user base: 1+ billion internet users
- Strong IM ecosystem: WeChat (1.3B), DingTalk (300M), Feishu (5M+), QQ (700M)
- Regulatory environment: Stricter AI governance and data privacy laws
- Tech talent: Abundant software engineers and AI researchers

### Key Market Opportunities

1. IM-based B2C automation (customer service, content moderation)
2. Enterprise automation (SME digital transformation)
3. Education tech (tutoring, assessment automation)
4. Financial services (robo-advisory, risk management)
5. Healthcare AI (diagnostics, drug discovery)

### Business Models for China Market

1. SaaS Subscription: Cloud-based Agent platform
2. Channel Partnership: Partnerships with cloud providers and integrators
3. Consulting Services: Agent customization and implementation
4. Skill Marketplace: Community-driven skills and tools monetization
