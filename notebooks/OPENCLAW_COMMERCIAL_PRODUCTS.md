# Commercial Products in OpenClaw Ecosystem

## Tier 1: Enterprise Platforms

### LobsterAI

- Target: Large enterprises in Finance, Healthcare, Manufacturing
- Pricing: Custom enterprise pricing, USD 100K-1M+ annually
- Features: Industry-specific templates, compliance, 24/7 support
- Go-to-market: Direct sales, partnerships with consultants

## Tier 2: Specialized Solutions

### QClaw (Quantum-Accelerated)

- Target: Financial institutions, research labs, optimization-heavy industries
- Pricing: Usage-based, per-query pricing (USD 0.01-1 per query)
- Features: Quantum acceleration, high-throughput processing

### Einclaw (Deep Learning Optimized)

- Target: AI researchers, computer vision teams, NLP specialists
- Pricing: Research license free, commercial USD 50K-500K annually
- Features: GPU optimization, model integration, research tools

## Tier 3: Low-Code/No-Code Solutions

### EasyClaw

- Target: Non-technical business users, SMEs, departments
- Pricing: Freemium (limited agents), USD 99-999/month per user
- Features: Drag-and-drop builder, pre-built templates, no coding
- Go-to-market: Direct to business, app stores, channel partners
